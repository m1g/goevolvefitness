Group Exercise Classes

HIIT Like An Athlete

HIIT Like An Athlete is a High Intensity Interval Training and Athletic Conditioning hybrid. It includes exercises such as burpees, squats, and push-ups with high intensity and staggered intervals to get your strength and metabolism up. The athletic conditioning piece includes the speed ladder, hurdles, and sprinting. All catered to your fitness level!

Personal Training

1-on-1 Personal and Small Group Training

For a more personalized approach to fitness, personal training sessions are available. Rob offers a strength training workout that will meet your fitness goals with an optional meal plan. These sessions are offered as individual or up to 3 people
